Induction Comuputing

Summary

This module covered the essentials required for getting the most out of your studies at Essex. The module gave an overview of the tools needed for computing studies, how to organise your learning focusing on areas such as time management and stress management. The second unit of the module was geared towards academic writing. I spent the majority of my time on this unit. As someone who has not been writing at an academic level for the last couple of years, I paid extra attention to learning the style of writing, Harvard referencing, and preparing for my induction assignment.
